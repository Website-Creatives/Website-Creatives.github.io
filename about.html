About Us

Website Creatives

We are a group of freelance web designers and developers
who specialise in working with start-ups and small businesses.

Who we are

We make simple (but awesome) websites for start-ups and new businesses. Every business needs a website -but they do not need to spend thousands on it. A website with your basic information can be more successful in bringing in more clients and customers than an all singing and dancing website that costs an arm and a leg to build (and maintain).

At Website Creatives we understand the costs of starting up and running your own business. Becoming self-employed is a daunting task and can be very expensive and time consuming.




About Joanna Janse van Vuuren

Website Creatives is led by designer and developer Joanna Janse van Vuuren. She has worked as a freelancer for over 5 years for local businesses and ecommerce stores, including an internship at The Prince's Trust.

"I am absolutely passionate about the internet, I see the internet as my home. With the ever rising levels of self-employment, new small business owners are recognising the value of having an online presence to increase their product and service awareness for their clients and customers, but they do not yet have the skills or funds to create a viable website. For years I have helped small business start their online presence and help them grow so they become self-sufficient online.”

During the evenings Joanna is Senior Instructor the local Taekwondo School as a 2nd Dan Black Belt Instructor using her degree in BA (hons) Sport and Leisure Management.
